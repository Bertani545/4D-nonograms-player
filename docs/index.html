4D nonograms

Use the next selectables to choose the dimensions of your puzzle. It can be of 2, 3 or 4 dimensions.

Please not that z = 1 or w = 1 is the same as if they didn't exists so they are dropped.

Please also note that a 10x10x10x10 puzzle contains 4000 lines to solve.



When you finish placing all the hints in a certain board, use the button Next Recomended Area to go to another board and continue completing the nonogram
